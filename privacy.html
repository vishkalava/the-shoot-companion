The Shoot Companion Privacy Policy Effective Date: July 7, 2026 The Shoot Companion is designed as a local-first application for macOS. This Privacy Policy explains how The Shoot Companion handles information when you use the application. Information We Collect The Shoot Companion does not require you to create an account or sign in. The Shoot Companion does not collect personal information for advertising, tracking, profiling, or marketing purposes. We do not sell personal information or user data. Local Data Storage Projects, scenes, shots, storyboard information, image editing settings, and other production information created within The Shoot Companion are stored locally on your Mac. This information is not automatically uploaded to servers operated by The Shoot Companion. You are responsible for maintaining appropriate backups of information stored on your Mac. Files and Storyboard Images The Shoot Companion may access images, documents, or other files that you explicitly select through macOS file selection dialogs. Selected files may be imported, copied, processed, or stored locally as part of your project or storyboard workflow. The Shoot Companion may also create exported files at locations that you explicitly select. The application does not automatically scan or access unrelated files stored on your Mac. Image Imports from URLs The Shoot Companion may connect to the internet when you explicitly request an image to be imported from a URL. The requested URL and standard network information required to establish the connection may be communicated to the website or server hosting that image. The Shoot Companion does not use URL imports for advertising or tracking. Third-party websites and servers are governed by their own privacy policies and practices. Local Network Communication The Shoot Companion may use your local network to communicate with compatible companion applications or devices, including applications designed to support production and on-set workflows. Local network communication may be used to exchange project, scene, shot, storyboard, capture, or related production information at your request. The Shoot Companion does not use local network communication for advertising or cross-app tracking. Data Sharing The Shoot Companion does not sell user data. The Shoot Companion does not share your project or production information with advertising companies or data brokers. Information may be transmitted to a third-party service only when you explicitly initiate a feature that requires communication with that service, such as importing an image from a URL. Analytics and Advertising The Shoot Companion does not use advertising SDKs. The Shoot Companion does not use your data for targeted advertising. The Shoot Companion does not track you across apps or websites for advertising purposes. User Accounts The Shoot Companion does not currently require a user account. No account profile is created when you use the application. Children's Privacy The Shoot Companion is a productivity and production-planning application and is not specifically directed toward children. The Shoot Companion does not knowingly collect personal information from children through an account-registration or advertising system. Data Retention and Deletion Because project and production information is stored locally on your Mac, you control the information created within The Shoot Companion. You may delete projects, scenes, shots, storyboard information, or other supported content through the application. Removing the application or deleting locally stored application data may also remove information stored by The Shoot Companion. You should maintain backups of important production information before deleting application data. Security The Shoot Companion uses the security and application sandboxing capabilities provided by macOS. However, no software or storage system can guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your Mac, user account, and locally stored project information. Changes to This Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated as The Shoot Companion develops or introduces new features. Any updated Privacy Policy will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
